Our Story

A Multi-Disciplinary
CA Firm Since 2014

VKS — Vijaywargi Khabiya & Saoji is a multi-disciplinary chartered accountancy firm based in Nagpur. Established in 2014 by three founding partners with deep domain expertise, the Firm has built a notable presence in a short span of time and grown to provide single-window professional services to organisations not only in Nagpur but also in Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Hyderabad, Indore and Raipur.

Our team of 80+ professionals comprises Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Advocates, IBBI-registered Insolvency Professionals and domain specialists — working collaboratively to deliver integrated, value-driven solutions tailored to each client's unique business context.

"Our approach is to combine our domain knowledge with the business goals of our clients. Given the ever-changing nature of law and policies, we are proactive and alert our clients of any update that may have an impact on their business."

— The VKS Philosophy

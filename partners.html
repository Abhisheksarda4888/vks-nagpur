Founding Partner

CA Varun Vijaywargi

FCA · Indirect Taxes & GST · 18+ Years Experience

CA Varun Vijaywargi is the first named Founding Partner at VKS. He specialises in Indirect Taxes & GST — covering advisory, compliance, litigation and setting-up businesses in India for domestic and foreign enterprises. With over 18 years of experience, he has guided large corporates, MSMEs and international companies through GST implementation, compliance optimisation, refund claims and complex litigation across multiple sectors.

Varun also leads the firm's practice on Setting-Up Businesses in India — advising foreign investors on entity structuring, FEMA/RBI compliance and full regulatory onboarding across sectors including IT, manufacturing and pharma.

Areas of Practice

GST Advisory & Litigation Indirect Tax Compliance GST Refund Claims Customs & DGFT FEMA & RBI Setting-Up in India GST Audit Foreign Investment

CA Pavan Khabiya

FCA · Audit & Direct Taxes · 18+ Years Experience

CA Pavan Khabiya specialises in Audit & Assurance and Direct Taxation. With over 18 years of professional experience, he has led statutory audit engagements for listed companies, banks, NBFCs and PSUs and is highly regarded for his meticulous approach to financial reporting, internal controls and regulatory compliance.

In the direct tax space, Pavan advises corporations on tax-efficient structures, handles assessments and reassessments, and has successfully represented clients before CIT(A) and ITAT. His sharp commercial understanding makes him a strategic advisor to CFOs and boards across industries.

Areas of Practice

Statutory Audit Internal Audit Tax Audit Direct Tax Advisory Transfer Pricing ITAT Representation Ind AS Transition Due Diligence

CA Ashish Saoji

FCA · Banking, IBC & Incentives · 18+ Years Experience

CA Ashish Saoji leads VKS's practice in Banking & Debt Syndication, Credit Ratings, State Incentives and Insolvency & Bankruptcy Code matters. With over 18 years of experience, he has handled landmark CIRP proceedings before the NCLT and has extensive expertise in creditor advisory, resolution planning and liquidation processes under IBC, 2016.

Ashish leads the firm's State Incentive practice — advising on PSI in Maharashtra, Telangana incentive schemes and structuring investments to maximise state-level benefits. His background in debt syndication integrates seamlessly with incentive and financing advisory.

Areas of Practice

Insolvency & IBC CIRP Proceedings Debt Syndication Credit Ratings State Incentives (PSI) NCLT Filings Creditor Advisory Project Finance

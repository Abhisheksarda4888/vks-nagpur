About the Firm

A Multi-Disciplinary
CA Firm Since 2014

VKS — Vijaywargi Khabiya & Saoji is a multi-disciplinary chartered accountancy firm based in Nagpur. Established in 2014, the Firm has built a notable presence and grown to provide single-window professional services to organisations not only in Nagpur but also in Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Hyderabad, Indore and Raipur.

Every Saturday, article assistants present on a topic from their current practice area — in front of the team and partners. It builds presentation skills, technical depth and professional confidence from Day 1.

Full study leave entitlement as prescribed by ICAI for CA Final examinations. We support your exam preparation — your success is our success.

06

Completion Certificate & Reference Letter

Upon successful completion, you receive your ICAI completion certificate, a detailed reference letter from the firm and a performance recommendation for placements.

How to Apply

1
Check Eligibility

You must have passed CA Intermediate (both groups) under the new ICAI curriculum. ICAI registration must be active and in order.

2
Send Your Application

Email your CV, mark sheets and ICAI registration details to us — or use the Apply button below to reach us via the contact form.

3
Interview at the Firm

Shortlisted candidates are called for a brief interaction at our Nagpur office. It's a conversation — not an interrogation. We want to know you.

4
Join & Begin Your Journey

Once selected, complete ICAI formalities, get your deed registered and join on your agreed date. Your VKS journey begins.
